What We Do

Our Specialist Services

From decorative artistry to structural protection — every stroke and seal is delivered with world-class craftsmanship.

Limewash Finishes

Authentic European limewash application creating depth, movement and that coveted old-world elegance to any interior or exterior wall.

Lime Plaster

Hand-applied, polished lime plaster for a seamless, luxurious surface. Venetian, Marmorino and Tadelakt finishes available.

Decorative Textures

Custom textured wall finishes, from rustic stone to modern geometric patterns — making every wall a statement piece.

Waterproofing

Complete waterproofing solutions for roofs, bathrooms, balconies and basements. Multi-layer membrane systems for lasting protection.

Residential Painting

Full interior and exterior painting for HDB, condos and landed properties. Premium paints, clean execution, zero hassle.

Commercial Projects

Large-scale painting and coating for offices, retail, F&B and industrial spaces. On-time, within budget, minimal disruption.

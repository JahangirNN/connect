About Vilaasita

Turning Houses Into Dream Homes Since Day One

At Vilaasita Interior, we believe that your home should be a reflection of your personality — elegant, comfortable, and uniquely yours. Based in Vadodara, Gujarat, we specialize in creating luxury interiors that blend royal aesthetics with modern comfort.

Our handcrafted sofas and bespoke furniture pieces are designed to be the centerpiece of your living spaces, where every curve and stitch tells a story of craftsmanship.

Nestled in the heart of the city, Velvet Roast is a sanctuary for coffee lovers. We source ethical beans, roast them to perfection, and serve them with intention.

Crafted with Intention

We believe coffee is more than caffeine—it's a ritual, a pause in the day, a moment of connection. Our beans are sourced directly from farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, ensuring fair trade practices and exceptional quality.

Every roast is profiled to highlight the unique characteristics of its origin. From floral Ethiopian Yirgacheffe to chocolatey Colombian Supremo, we celebrate the diversity of flavor that coffee has to offer.

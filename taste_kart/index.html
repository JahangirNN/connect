Our Story

The Art of Healthy Living

Founded by Chef Neeta Vinaykiya, Taste Kart was born out of a simple desire: to make dessert healthy again. In a world full of refined sugars and flours, we chose the harder path—the path of quality.

From our professional culinary classes to our bespoke orders, every interaction with Taste Kart is a step towards a more conscious, joyful way of eating.

What is Ayurveda?

The term Ayurveda is derived from the Sanskrit words ayur (life) and veda (science or knowledge). Thus, Ayurveda translates to knowledge of life.

At Eira Ayurveda, we embrace this philosophy to help you achieve balance and longevity through natural healing and holistic practices.
